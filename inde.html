About me

Ahoj a vitaj! I'm Ingrid.

Most people learn Slovak for very personal reasons — love and family. That's why I started teaching my mother tongue: to help you feel at home in Slovak, not just in lessons but in your relationships and everyday life.

I'm a native Slovak speaker with a Master's in Translation and Interpreting (English and French) and years of experience teaching in language schools. But don't let the academic side fool you — my lessons are relaxed, friendly and practical. We'll laugh a lot and focus on the real conversations that matter to you.

I especially love working with:

  • people with a Slovak partner who want to talk with the family,
  • people with Slovak roots who want to connect with their culture.

Good to know

Questions, answered.

Do I need to know any Slovak already?

Not a word. Many of my students start as complete beginners. If you already know some — say from a Slovak parent or partner — we'll pick up right where you are.

How do the lessons work?

We meet online over video call, one-to-one. Every lesson is interactive and built around you: your goals, your pace, and the real situations you want to use Slovak in.

I'm learning for my partner's family. Can you help with that?

Absolutely — it's one of my two specialities. We'll focus on the real conversations you'll have: greetings, family gatherings, traditions, and the little phrases that win hearts.

What if I'm nervous or have tried before and given up?

You're in exactly the right place. My lessons are warm and pressure-free — no being made to feel silly, ever. We go at your speed and celebrate small wins.

How do I book?

Easiest is to pick a time yourself with the "Book a free trial lesson" button — it opens my calendar and you choose a slot. You can also message me on WhatsApp or email unlockslovak@gmail.com and we'll find a time together.
